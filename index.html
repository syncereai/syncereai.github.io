Domestic Robots for Housekeeping

Syncere's goal is to automate physical work, freeing humans from demanding tasks. We see robots as labor multipliers that can help people work less and achieve more. To make this possible, we're starting in hotels by building robots to learn and perform housekeeping tasks.

We are a team of PhD roboticists and AI researchers from the University of Toronto.

If you’re a hotel owner or manager interested in integrating robotic solutions into your property, please reach out to us at hello@syncereai.com.
