SYNCERE

Furniture that does your chores.

At Syncere, we think the future of domestic robotics is ambient. Robots should blend into your home, not take it over.

To achieve this, we are building robot technologies that integrates with furniture in your home. No eye sores. No roaming machines. No new ways of interacting.

Our goal is to automate your chores without adding humanoids to your home, because we believe your home should be for your family - not shared with robot roommates.

Our team is in the early stages of building, and includes PhDs and engineers from Stanford, University of Toronto, Figure, AMD, and more.
