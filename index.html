SYNCERE

Robots that feel at home, in your home.

At Syncere, we believe domestic robots should serve quietly by being almost invisible in the background.

They should feel familiar, as if they've always belonged while also complementing the things you already love without disrupting the feeling of home.

This is why we built Lume.

Lume is a pair of robotic lamps that live by your bed. Most of the time, they are just that. But when needed, they transform into robotic arms that fold your laundry.

Just toss your clothes on the bed and walk away. When you return, everything is folded.
