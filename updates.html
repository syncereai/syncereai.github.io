January 2025

This month, we focused on improving the hardware and developing software for autonomous cleaning. Early in January, we continued iterating on the robotic arm to make it more robust and reliable. We updated our build document here. By mid-month, we finally got a version of our prototype that was robust enough to complete tasks autonomously, so we began working on the software for bathroom cleaning. By the end of the month, we were able to autonomously clean the seat of a toilet, as well as perform garbage disposal (demo).

December 2024

This month, we made progress in developing our robotic arm, from achieving 4 DoF motion to solving inverse kinematics for precise operations. Early in December, we motorized the arm and demonstrated its 4 DoF capability (demo). The founders also worked with the Toronto Pearson Marriott team to learn about their housekeeping workflows (photo). Midway through the month, we completed a technical report detailing our roadmap. We continued to make improvements to the arm’s vertical range and repeatability (demo). By the end of December, we successfully solved the inverse kinematics, which allowed the robot to perform a precise pick-and-place task (demo).

November 2024

This month, we laid the foundation for our robotic arm project by building tech demos, designing hardware, and planning the next stages of development. Early in November, we completed our PhDs and showcased the potential of our technology through demos, including using VLMs to locate objects in a hotel lobby (demo) and enabling natural language, gesture, and external media controls (demo). By mid-month, we conducted interviews with housekeepers and operations staff at a Marriott to understand their workflows. Simultaneously, we began developing software for autonomous robot manipulator control for tasks like cleaning mirrors (demo) and designed our first hardware prototype (demo). By the end of November, we 3D-printed the first set of parts, received the electronics, and began assembling our first hardware prototype (demo).
